About Us

Treats of Rithu is a dedicated wholesale supplier specializing in high-quality bakery products. Known for its freshness, consistency, and taste, the unit caters to bakeries, cafes, parties, retail stores, and bulk customers by providing a wide range of items such as cakes, pastries, breads, cookies, and customized baked goods.

With a strong focus on quality ingredients and hygienic preparation, Treats of Rithu ensures that every product meets high standards while maintaining affordable pricing for bulk orders. The brand aims to be a reliable partner for businesses by delivering delicious baked products on time and in large quantities.
